# 9. Issues Encountered

- **Issue**: Jest timeout of 15 seconds was exceeded when spawning the Python forecasting model in the backend during the `POST /api/forecasts` test. This was due to `statsmodels.tsa.arima.model.ARIMA` training load.
- **Resolution**: Modified `app/tests/api.test.js` to apply a 30,000ms timeout parameter specifically to the forecasting test. This prevents flaky timeout exceptions on slower CPU runner environments.
