# 11. Lessons Learned

1. Matplotlib Agg backend must be set before any other import to avoid display errors on headless systems
2. On-Time Delivery analysis requires filtering cancelled and pending POs before computing the rate
3. Structuring charts by business domain (sales, products, etc.) dramatically improves discoverability for stakeholders
4. Correlation heatmap with annotated cell values is more actionable than an unlabeled heatmap
5. Business insight structure (Observation/Evidence/Impact/Recommendation/Priority/Benefit) is the gold standard for executive reporting
