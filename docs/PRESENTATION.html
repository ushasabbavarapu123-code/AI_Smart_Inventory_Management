Business Intelligence

Actionable Insights & Recommendations

Derived from EDA, feature importance analysis, and supply chain optimization modelling across 50 SKUs and 914 transactions.

📐 Dynamic Safety Stock & Reorder Points

Replace static reorder rules with formula-driven dynamic values:

SS = Z × σ_demand × √(Lead Time)
ROP = (Avg Daily Demand × Lead Time) + SS

➡ Maintains a 95% service level (Z=1.645), cutting stockout risk while reducing holding costs.

📈 Volatility-Based Stock Buffers

Products with high rolling_std_30 scores (Electronics, seasonal apparel) need 25–40% higher safety stock multipliers to absorb demand spikes.

🤝 Supplier Performance Optimization

Prioritize suppliers with:

< 7
Lead time (days)
> 4.0
Rating score (/5)

➡ Reduces ROP lead time factor → leaner, cost-efficient inventory buffers.

🔄 Continuous Forecasting Loop

Schedule weekly model retraining with model_optimization.py to capture seasonal shifts, promotion effects, and post-event demand corrections — maintaining forecast accuracy over time.
